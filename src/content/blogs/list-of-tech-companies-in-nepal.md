When the world thinks of Nepal, the mind naturally drifts to the snowy peaks of Mount Everest, ancient temples, and winding trekking routes. It rarely pictures high-end code being shipped to Boeing, AI models being fine-tuned for Microsoft, or a fintech sector processing **$25 billion annually**.

Yet, a quiet digital revolution has transformed Kathmandu into one of South Asia's most intriguing tech hubs. This is no longer just a destination for low-cost outsourcing; the narrative has shifted to high-value innovation. Nepal’s tech sector now generates over **$500 million in exports**, pivoting rapidly into specialized fields like Artificial Intelligence, healthcare analytics, and cybersecurity.

Whether you are a global investor scouting the next untapped talent pool, or a tech professional seeking a career pivot, these are the companies defining Nepal's digital landscape in 2025.

---

## 1. The Global Powerhouses
*The heavy hitters connecting Kathmandu directly to Silicon Valley and beyond.*

These companies have established the "gold standard" for engineering in Nepal. They don't just execute tasks; they build the core architecture for massive global enterprises.

### Leapfrog Technology
**Link:** [lftechnology.com](https://lftechnology.com)


**Focus:** Healthcare Tech, AI, & Digital Health
As a powerhouse US-based firm with over **450 staff** in Nepal, Leapfrog has brought a true Silicon Valley-style work culture to the Himalayas. They are not a typical dev shop; they partner with high-growth startups to build advanced healthcare tech and AI solutions. Their engineering expertise is deep, often credited with saving clients millions through innovative platform architecture.

### Cotiviti Nepal
**Link:** [cotiviti.com](https://cotiviti.com)


**Focus:** US Healthcare Informatics & Payment Accuracy
One of the largest employers in the region, Cotiviti houses over **600 engineers** focused on high-stakes US healthcare data. They specialize in payment accuracy analytics—a critical field where precision is non-negotiable. Despite occasional regulatory challenges, they remain a premier destination for top talent, offering exposure to massive datasets that few other companies can match.


### Cedar Gate Technologies
**Link:** [cedargate.com](https://cedargate.com)


**Focus:** Value-Based Healthcare Analytics
Following the high-profile acquisition of Deerwalk, Cedar Gate has cemented its place as a leader in the healthcare domain. With over **700 professionals**, they build sophisticated platforms specifically for "value-based care." Their software allows US healthcare providers to manage financial risk and improve patient care quality, making them a critical player in the American medical infrastructure.

### CloudFactory
**Link:** [cloudfactory.com](https://cloudfactory.com)


**Focus:** Human-in-the-Loop AI & Machine Learning
CloudFactory is unique. They don't just write code; they power the data that makes AI smart. As a global leader in "human-in-the-loop" processing, they connect a massive distributed workforce in Nepal with tech giants like **Microsoft** and autonomous vehicle companies. They prepare the high-quality, complex datasets required to train the world's most advanced machine learning models.

### Gurzu
**Link:** [gurzu.com](https://gurzu.com)


**Focus:** Agile Product Engineering
Gurzu has carved out a niche by saying "no" to generic, low-end outsourcing. They represent the modern wave of specialized engineering firms, building high-quality digital products for ambitious startups in the **US and Australia**. Their reputation is built on strict adherence to Agile methodologies and delivering complex, custom software solutions.

## 2. The Homegrown Innovators (Product & Fintech)
*The companies building the digital rails for Nepal’s economy.*

While the companies above look outward, these companies look inward, revolutionizing how the Nepalese people transact and interact with their government.

### F1Soft International
**Link:** [f1soft.com](https://f1soft.com) | [esewa.com.np](https://esewa.com.np)


**Focus:** Fintech Ecosystem Leader
F1Soft is the undisputed heavyweight champion of Nepal’s domestic tech scene. As the parent company of **eSewa**, they control the ubiquitous digital wallet serving over **26 million users**. Their influence is staggering—their software infrastructure powers nearly **90%** of the country's financial institutions.

### Khalti
**Link:** [khalti.com](https://khalti.com)


**Focus:** Digital Payments & API Integration
If F1Soft is the established giant, Khalti is the aggressive, youth-focused challenger. Known for vibrant branding and seamless API integrations, they have expanded the definition of a "digital wallet." Beyond simple utility payments, Khalti has digitized the lifestyle sector, covering movies, events, and government services, rapidly winning over the younger demographic.


### InfoDevelopers
**Link:** [infodev.com.np](https://infodev.com.np)


**Focus:** Core Banking & Government Tech
While less consumer-facing than eSewa or Khalti, InfoDevelopers is the "quiet backbone" of the financial sector. They dominate the Core Banking System (CBS) market, serving over **1,500 cooperatives and microfinance institutions**. Furthermore, they are a key player in the public sector, digitizing crucial government services and building the tech infrastructure that runs the nation.

## 3. Specialized Leaders (AI, Security & Infrastructure)
*The niche experts tackling complex global problems.*

This sector highlights the maturity of the ecosystem, moving beyond general web development into high-stakes security and cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence.

### Fusemachines
**Link:** [fusemachines.com](https://fusemachines.com)


**Focus:** Enterprise AI & Education
Founded by a Nepali-American entrepreneur with a mission to "democratize AI," Fusemachines offers high-end enterprise solutions. However, their true legacy may be their **"AI Fellowship" program**, which has created a sustainable pipeline of machine learning engineers in Nepal, readying local talent for the global market.

### Noble Stack
**Link:** [noblestack.io](https://noblestack.io)


**Focus:** LLMs, NLP, & AI Agents
A rising star in the AI landscape, Noble Stack is bridging the gap between modern web frameworks (Next.js) and advanced **Large Language Models (LLMs)**. They are pioneering local Natural Language Processing (NLP) by developing the first **"Nepali Grammarly,"** a tool poised to assist over 10 million native speakers. Simultaneously, they are deploying autonomous AI agents and automation tools for international clients.

### Vairav Technology
**Link:** [vairav.net](https://vairav.net)


**Focus:** Managed Security Services (SOC)
As Nepal digitizes its banking and government sectors, the attack surface grows. Vairav Technology has stepped up as a leading homegrown cybersecurity firm. Operating a dedicated **Security Operations Center (SOC)** in Kathmandu, they are critical in protecting national data infrastructure from emerging cyber threats.

### LogPoint Nepal
**Link:** [logpoint.com](https://logpoint.com)


**Focus:** Cybersecurity (SIEM)
LogPoint Nepal serves as the key R&D hub for the Copenhagen-based cybersecurity leader. They specialize in **Security Information and Event Management (SIEM)**. Using advanced AI to detect threats, they are aggressively expanding into network detection and response, bolstered by recent international acquisitions.

### WorldLink Communications
**Link:** [worldlink.com.np](https://worldlink.com.np)


**Focus:** Internet Infrastructure (ISP)
None of the companies on this list could exist without WorldLink. As Nepal’s largest ISP with a **35% market share**, they provide the infrastructure layer for the entire ecosystem. Backed by international investment, they are currently building Tier III data centers and aggressively expanding fiber connectivity to rural areas, bridging the digital divide.

## 4. Notable High-Growth Startups
*The agile disruptors proving Nepal can export products, not just labor.*

### Programiz
**Link:** [programiz.com](https://programiz.com)


**Focus:** Global EdTech
Ranked as the **#1 startup in Nepal by web traffic**, Programiz is a global powerhouse providing programming tutorials to millions of users worldwide. They are living proof that Nepal can export high-quality educational content and build a massive global audience, rather than just writing backend code for someone else.

### Alaya (formerly HLE Nepal)
**Link:** [alaya.co](https://alaya.co)


**Focus:** Mortgage Tech & Work Culture
Alaya services the Australian mortgage industry, but they are most famous for transforming the local work culture. With a rare **5-day work week** and morning shifts (6 AM - 2 PM), they prioritize work-life balance and boast incredibly high employee retention rates in a volatile market.

### Braindigit
**Link:** [braindigit.com](https://braindigit.com)


**Focus:** Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)
A veteran player in the enterprise space, Braindigit focuses on digitizing SMEs both locally and globally. They offer end-to-end digital transformation services and maintain a significant footprint in the domestic enterprise software market.

### EB Pearls
**Link:** [ebpearls.com.au](https://ebpearls.com.au)


**Focus:** Mobile App Development & UI/UX
An Australian-owned heavy hitter, EB Pearls operates a massive development hub in Kathmandu. They are the go-to firm for high-end UI/UX and mobile solutions, having successfully delivered over **600 ecommerce and mobile projects**.

### The Verdict?
Nepal is open for business. The transition from a "low-cost alternative" to a "high-value partner" is well underway. With a growing pool of engineers fluent in AI, English, and modern tech stacks, Kathmandu is poised to become a significant node in the global digital economy.
